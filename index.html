The top 5 football games

Posted on February 9, 2024 by Owen Silberman

1. 1982 NFC Championship: San Francisco 49ers vs. Dallas Cowboys

Known for "The Catch," this game solidified Joe Montana's legacy and sent the 49ers to their first Super Bowl.

2. Super Bowl LI: New England Patriots vs. Atlanta Falcons, 2017

The Patriots overcame a 28-3 deficit to beat the Falcons in overtime, marking the largest comeback in Super Bowl history.

3. Super Bowl XLII: New York Giants vs. New England Patriots, 2008

The Giants ended the Patriots' quest for a perfect season, highlighted by the miraculous "Helmet Catch" by David Tyree.

4. 1974 "Sea of Hands" Game: Miami Dolphins vs. Oakland Raiders

This AFC Divisional Playoff game is famous for its dramatic ending, with Ken Stabler's last-minute touchdown pass to Clarence Davis.

5. 1994 AFC Championship: San Diego Chargers vs. Pittsburgh Steelers

An underdog victory that sent the Chargers to their first Super Bowl, this game is remembered for its intense fourth quarter.

Top 5 basketball games

Posted on February 9, 2024 by Owen Silberman

1. 1980 NBA Finals: Los Angeles Lakers vs. Philadelphia 76ers

Game 6 of the 1980 NBA Finals is known for Magic Johnson's performance, as he played all five positions and led the Lakers to victory.

2. 1998 NBA Finals: Chicago Bulls vs. Utah Jazz

Michael Jordan's game-winning shot in Game 6 secured the Bulls' sixth championship in eight years, capping off his legendary career with the team.

3. 1970 NBA Finals: New York Knicks vs. Los Angeles Lakers

Game 7 of the 1970 NBA Finals is remembered for Willis Reed's dramatic return from injury, which inspired the Knicks to victory.

4. 1987 Eastern Conference Semifinals: Boston Celtics vs. Detroit Pistons

Game 5 of this series is known as the "Bird steals the ball" game, as Larry Bird's late-game steal and assist helped the Celtics secure the win.

5. 2006 NBA Finals: Miami Heat vs. Dallas Mavericks

Dwyane Wade's performance in Game 3, where he scored 42 points and led the Heat to victory, is considered one of the greatest individual performances in NBA Finals history.

Top 5 soccer games

Posted on February 9, 2024 by Owen Silberman

1. 1953 Hungary vs. England

Known as the "Match of the Century," Hungary's 6-3 victory over England at Wembley Stadium marked the end of England's unbeaten home record and solidified Hungary's status as the top team in the world.

2. 1970 World Cup: Italy vs. West Germany

The 1970 World Cup semifinal between Italy and West Germany is remembered for its dramatic overtime period, with Italy ultimately securing a 4-3 victory and advancing to the final.

3. 1999 UEFA Champions League Final: Manchester United vs. Bayern Munich

Manchester United's late comeback, with two goals in injury time, secured a 2-1 victory and completed a historic treble for the club.

4. 2014 World Cup: Brazil vs. Germany

Germany's 7-1 victory over Brazil in the 2014 World Cup semifinal is considered one of the most shocking results in the history of the tournament.

5. 2005 UEFA Champions League Final: Liverpool vs. AC Milan

Liverpool's comeback from a 3-0 deficit at halftime to win the match on penalties is considered one of the greatest comebacks in the history of the sport.

Top 5 baseball games

Posted on February 9, 2024 by Owen Silberman

1. 1975 World Series: Boston Red Sox vs. Cincinnati Reds

Game 6 of the 1975 World Series is remembered for Carlton Fisk's dramatic walk-off home run in the 12th inning, which forced a Game 7 and secured the Red Sox's place in baseball history.

2. 1951 National League Playoff: New York Giants vs. Brooklyn Dodgers

Bobby Thomson's "Shot Heard 'Round the World" in the bottom of the ninth inning secured a come-from-behind victory for the Giants and sent them to the World Series.

3. 2001 World Series: New York Yankees vs. Arizona Diamondbacks

Game 7 of the 2001 World Series is remembered for Luis Gonzalez's walk-off single against Mariano Rivera, which secured the Diamondbacks' first championship in franchise history.

4. 1986 World Series: New York Mets vs. Boston Red Sox

Game 6 of the 1986 World Series is known for Bill Buckner's error, which allowed the Mets to win the game and ultimately the series.

5. 2016 World Series: Chicago Cubs vs. Cleveland Indians

Game 7 of the 2016 World Series is remembered for its dramatic extra innings, with the Cubs ultimately securing their first championship in over 100 years.

Top 5 hockey games

Posted on February 9, 2024 by Owen Silberman

1. 1980 Winter Olympics: United States vs. Soviet Union

The "Miracle on Ice" game, where the underdog United States team defeated the heavily favored Soviet Union, is considered one of the greatest upsets in sports history.

2. 1994 Stanley Cup Finals: New York Rangers vs. Vancouver Canucks

Game 7 of the 1994 Stanley Cup Finals is remembered for the Rangers' victory, which secured their first championship in 54 years.

3. 1972 Summit Series: Canada vs. Soviet Union

The 1972 Summit Series, where Canada defeated the Soviet Union in a dramatic eight-game series, is considered a defining moment in international hockey.

4. 2010 Winter Olympics: Canada vs. United States

The gold medal game of the 2010 Winter Olympics, where Canada defeated the United States in overtime, is remembered for its intense competition and dramatic finish.

5. 1987 Canada Cup: Canada vs. Soviet Union

The final game of the 1987 Canada Cup, where Canada defeated the Soviet Union in a thrilling three-game series, is considered one of the greatest displays of international hockey talent.
